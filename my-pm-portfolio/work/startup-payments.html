Payments & Onboarding Platform

Enabling small businesses to sign up and transact with ease

Context

A rapidly growing fintech startup wanted to simplify how small and medium‑sized enterprises (SMEs) sign up for financial services. Existing processes were paper‑heavy, slow and prone to errors, leading to high drop‑off rates.

Goals

  • Reduce onboarding time from days to minutes.
  • Provide an intuitive payments dashboard for merchants.
  • Ensure regulatory compliance across multiple jurisdictions.

Approach

  1. User research & segmentation: interviewed merchants to understand their pain points and technical proficiency.
  2. Prototyping & usability testing: iterated on onboarding flows and dashboards using high‑fidelity prototypes.
  3. Incremental rollout: launched the platform to a pilot group, capturing feedback and iterating before full release.

Results

Metric Before After Unit
Onboarding time 5 0.5 days
Merchant retention 70 90 %
Transaction volume – ↑200% quarterly

Impact

The platform enabled thousands of SMEs to accept payments quickly and securely. The streamlined onboarding increased conversion rates, and the transparent dashboard improved trust and engagement.

Next Steps

We plan to expand the platform to include invoicing, analytics and integrations with accounting software.
