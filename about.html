About Me

Hi, I'm Oluwafemi (friends call me Femi). I'm a technology product manager and recreational tennis player living in Calgary, Alberta. I began my career in finance and pivoted to product management after discovering my passion for solving problems with technology.

Off the court and outside of work, I picked up tennis at 30 and have been chasing improvement ever since. It's a hobby that keeps me sharp and humble. I also share my life with my wonderful wife, whose support and love ground everything I do.

Over the years I've led cross‑functional teams through the entire product lifecycle, from discovery and hypothesis formulation to delivery and iteration. I care deeply about aligning products with user needs and business outcomes, and I love helping organizations transition toward product‑led cultures.

Values & Principles

  • Empathy: Always start with understanding people—customers, teammates and stakeholders.
  • Curiosity: Embrace ambiguity, ask questions and never stop learning.
  • Integrity: Build trust through transparency and accountability.
  • Collaboration: Great products emerge from diverse perspectives and shared ownership.

How I Work

I bring structure to chaos by breaking big problems into manageable pieces. My process typically looks like:

  1. Frame the problem & identify our target user.
  2. Map the journey & surface pain points.
  3. Co‑create solutions with designers and engineers.
  4. Deliver a minimal viable product and iterate based on data.
